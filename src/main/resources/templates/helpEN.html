About this app

Because of the busy life that we all live these days, we made an app that will surely brighten your day. This app finds the nearest pharmacy to you, it finds the pharmacy that works at the moment when you use the app and it shows the pharmacies you can go to. When you would allow access to your location, we will show you the route to your nearest pharmacy.
Warning: This app contains the map of municipality Karpos in Skopje and the pharmacies in its territory.

If you are using the app, maybe this could help you

  1. Please let us use your personal private location. (By the laws of the Republic of North Macedonia, your location will be only used for the functionalities of this app)
  2. If you click on "Pharmacies", it will show a list of pharmacies on the territory of Karpos municipality
  3. If you click on "Map",the app will generate the nearest pharmacy to you and the route to get there
  4. If you click on the logo, it will guide you to the home page of this website
